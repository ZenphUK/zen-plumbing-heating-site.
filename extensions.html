Types of Extensions We Build

Every home is different. We design and build the extension that fits your property, your budget, and your lifestyle.

Single Storey Rear Extension

The most popular choice for Kent families. Extends your home into the garden to create a larger kitchen, dining area, or open-plan living space. Often possible under Permitted Development.

Perfect for: Kitchen-diners, family rooms, garden connection

Side Return Extension

That narrow alleyway down the side of your house? It’s wasted space. A side return fills it in, often transforming a small kitchen into a wide, usable room without eating up your garden.

Perfect for: Victorian & terraced homes, wider kitchens

Wraparound Extension

Combines a rear and side extension for maximum impact. Creates stunning L-shaped open-plan spaces with room for kitchen islands, dining zones, and living areas — all flooded with natural light.

Perfect for: Complete ground floor transformation
