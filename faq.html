What services do you provide?

We provide plumbing, Gas Safe, boilers and heating, bathrooms, house and apartments repairs, underfloor heating, and selected larger home improvement works including loft conversion and extension-related services where appropriate.

Do you work with homeowners only?

No. We also work with landlords, letting agents, property contacts, and other local partners where the job is a good fit.

How do I leave a review?

The easiest way is through our Google Business Profile. We can send you a direct review link after the job is complete. Public review link: Leave a Google review.

Can I ask about more than one type of work in the same enquiry?

Yes. Many jobs overlap, so one message or call is enough to start the conversation properly.

Can you help if I am still at the planning stage?

Yes. Early conversations often lead to better decisions, especially for bathrooms, renovations, extensions, and heating upgrades.

Do you offer Gas Safe work?

Yes. Gas work is covered through our dedicated Gas Safe route, including servicing, installation, testing, and relevant records where applicable.

Which areas do you cover?

We cover DA1-DA18 and surrounding areas including Dartford, Gravesend, Bexley, Greenhithe, Ebbsfleet, Meopham, Sidcup, and nearby locations.

What is the best way to contact you?

If the matter is urgent, calling is best. For planned work, a callback request or WhatsApp message is often the easiest route.
