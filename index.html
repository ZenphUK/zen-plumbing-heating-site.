Meet the owner

Hi, I’m Zen, the owner of Zen Plumbing & Heating.

For over 15 years I’ve been working hands‑on in homes across Gravesend, Northfleet, Dartford, Ebbsfleet and the surrounding Kent towns. I didn’t start this business from behind a desk — I started it on building sites, in kitchens, bathrooms and living rooms, learning exactly what homeowners need and, more importantly, what they shouldn’t have to put up with.

That’s why, when you call Zen Plumbing & Heating, you won’t be passed around a call center or left talking to someone who doesn’t know the job. You speak directly with me. I personally oversee every project, answer your questions, and make sure the work is done properly from start to finish.

I’ve built my team around one simple rule: we treat your home like our own. That means showing up on time, respecting your space, cleaning up after ourselves, and never cutting corners. Every person who works with us is trained to my standards — not because I’m difficult, but because I believe your home deserves the same care I’d give my own.

I also believe in honesty from day one. No hidden costs. No nasty surprises. Before we start, you’ll know exactly what’s included, how long it will take, and what it will cost. If anything changes, we discuss it with you first.

Whether you need a small repair, a full renovation, a new kitchen or bathroom, or help getting a property ready to let or sell, we’re here to make it straightforward and stress‑free.

If that sounds like the kind of company you’d like to work with, I’d love to hear from you.
